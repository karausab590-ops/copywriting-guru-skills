# Mark Build Brand Foundation Docs

## Overview
This skill helps you create the 4 essential foundational documents that must be completed BEFORE writing any advertorial, sales page, VSL, or marketing copy.

**Keywords**: foundation docs, avatar sheet, offer brief, necessary beliefs, customer research, Todd Brown, StoryBrand

## The 4 Foundation Documents

### 1. Avatar Sheet
- Demographics, Psychographics
- Goals & Desires (surface + deep)
- Pain Points (top 5)
- Objections, Current State, Dream State
- Awareness Level, Influences, Language

### 2. Offer Brief
- Product Overview, Target Market
- The Offer + Unique Mechanism
- Proof & Credibility
- Competitive Landscape
- Customer Journey, Emotional Triggers
- Key Selling Points

### 3. Necessary Beliefs (Todd Brown E5)
6 beliefs customer must hold before buying:
1. Market Belief
2. Competition Belief
3. Credibility Belief
4. Mechanism Belief
5. Proof Belief
6. Urgency Belief

### 4. Summary
One-page synthesis of everything.

## Workflow
1. Research Phase (forums, reviews, competitor ads)
2. Avatar Sheet (60-90 min)
3. Offer Brief (45-60 min)
4. Necessary Beliefs (60-90 min)
5. Summary (30 min)

## When to Use
- Before any advertorial or sales page
- New product launch
- When existing copy isn't converting
